Homemade Chocolate Ice Cream

Let's make chocolate ice cream

This is the best Chocalte Ice Cream. Here is what you need!

  • 2 cups heavy cream divided
  • ¼ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 6 ounces semisweet chocolate chopped (or chocolate chips)
  • 1 ½ cups whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ⅛ tsp sea salt
  • 3 large egg yolks beaten
  • ½ tsp pure vanilla extract

Now you have your ingredients, let's go! Step by step:

  1. In a saucepan, heat 1 cup heavy cream with cocoa powder, stirring until it just begins to simmer
  2. Remove from heat and add chopped chocolate, stirring until fully melted and smooth
  3. Pour in the remaining 1 cup of cream and mix until combined, then set aside
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks until slightly thickened
  5. In another saucepan, warm the milk, sugar, and a pinch of salt over medium heat
  6. Slowly add some of the warm milk mixture to the egg yolks while whisking, then pour it all back into the saucepan
  7. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens enough to coat the back of a spoon
  8. Strain the custard into the chocolate mixture to remove any lumps, then stir everything together
  9. Add vanilla extract and mix well
  10. Cover and refrigerate for several hours until completely chilled
  11. Churn in an ice cream maker until it reaches a thick, creamy consistency
  12. Transfer to a container and freeze until firm
  13. Enjoy your homemade chocolate ice cream!
