Banana Split

Let's make a Banana Split

This is one of my favorite desserts, but it is important to know that the ingredients make the difference here! You want to pick or make your favorite ice creams and toppings. Here are my suggestions:

  • Your FAVORITE Chocolate, Vanilla, and Strawberry ice cream. Try our Homemade Recipes!
  • Carmel (homemade is best) but I love the Letherbys carmel if you can get your hands on it
  • Chocolate syrup or fudge. Dealers choice!
  • Fresh strawberry compote. Mmmm.
  • Your favorite whipped cream or cool whip
  • Nuts and a cherry!

Now you have your ingredients, let's go! Step by step:

  1. Scoop one scoop of Vanilla, Chocolate, and Strawberrry ice cream into a dish
  2. Add a scoop of strawberry compote to the strawberry ice cream
  3. Add a scoop of carmel to the vanilla ice cream
  4. Add a scoop of chocolate syrup to the chocolate ice cream
  5. Top the whole thing with fresh whipped cream
  6. Add a sprinkle of nuts and a cherry on top
  7. Enjoy!!!
